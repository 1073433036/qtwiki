Qt Ecosystem Team Task List

This is the task list of the Qt Ecosystem team. Our objective is to promote usage of Qt outside Nokia.

Our work can be categorized as below:

  1. Ongoing work we have already committed to
  2. Complete QtMediaHub. QtMediaHub is our primary vehicle to showcase Qt and QML
  3. Empower externals to contribute to Qt project
  4. Have Qt run fantastically on 2 boards (Raspberry Pi and BCM 7425 are the candidates)
  5. Contribute to missing components in Qt that make it interesting for non-Nokia
