Qt for Tizen

Contact or join us! Users of Qt for Tizen can join application-dev@lists.tizen.org mailing list.

If you want develop the project subscribe to development dev@lists.tizen.org mailing list.

Hi! Qt for Tizen is a purely community-driven port of Qt 5 to Tizen smartphones, cars, home appliances, tablets and notebooks.
