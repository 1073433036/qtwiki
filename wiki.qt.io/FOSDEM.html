FOSDEM = Free and Open Source Developers' European Meeting

Edition 2012 will happen on Feb 4 and 5, 2012

Address: ULB Campus Solbosh Avenue Franklin D. Roosevelt, 50 1050 Bruxelles

http://www.fosdem.org

Schedule

  • Saturday 12:30-13:30, Towards Accelerated UI's on Mobile Linux With Power of Qt5, room AW1.120, by Saija Eteläniemi
  • Saturday 14:00-14:30, Deep Dive into KDE Mobile development on N9/N950 (Harmattan), room AW1.120, by Laszlo Papp
  • Saturday 14:30-15:00, Community Qt apps repository - way forward, room AW1.120, by Jukka Eklund
  • Saturday 16:00-16:30, Introduction to the QtonPi project, room AW1.120, by Rajiv Ranganath
  • Sunday 10:00-10:40, Toolkits on Wayland - how we're doing!, room H.1308, by Rob Bradford and Tiago Vignatti
  • Sunday 11:40-12:30, qt.io and Qt 5, room H.1308, by Lars Knoll and Thiago Macieira
  • Sunday 13:00-14:00, Using Qt for non-graphical applications, room Lameere, by Thomas Petazzoni
